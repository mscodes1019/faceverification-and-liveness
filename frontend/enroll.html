What is a face template?

A face template is a unique set of numbers that represent the distinctive features of your face.

If you choose to enroll, you'll take a few photos of your face today to create one.

Using face templates helps verify identity quickly and consistently in this demo workflow.

What information gets stored?

Only your face template gets stored. No one has access to your face template.

We protect your privacy at every step

Face templates are used only for enrollment demonstration

Your face template isn't shared or used outside this approved demo purpose.

Face templates are securely encrypted

Your face template is encrypted following cloud security standards ISO 27018 and SOC 1, 2, 3.

Retention follows the selected enrollment policy

Raw photos are discarded unless retention is explicitly enabled.

Delete your data anytime

You can change your mind anytime and delete your face template.

Tips for creating a face template

The photo will be taken automatically once your face is detected in position — no button needed. These photos will only be used to create your face template and will not be stored.

The camera works best when your face is fully visible and the photos show how you look on a typical day.

Look straight ahead and center your face in the oval guide.

Make sure you're the only person in view.

Some accessories can obscure parts of your face, such as a cap, sunglasses, or a face mask, so you might need to adjust these while taking the photos.
